Who am I ?

I'm a french progammer born in 1986. My first interest is of course Open Source, as described by the FSF, and evangelization of companies. At work, I'm an embedded engineer which target double competence Hardware/Software (I know this is hard to achieve).

I'm an ESEO alumni and got my diploma in September 2009. For more information about me use my social links.

Current Work

I'm working for the Scilab Consortium. I have to maintain and develop the Xcos/Scicos component of the Scilab software.

I have to extend the Xcos module and the Scilab platform with code generation features.

Past position 

2009

I was working at Geensys, on the AutosarLab department. My mission was to port an Open Source TCP/IP stack on the AUTOSAR layered architecture. To achieve the goal, I had to complete some AUTOSAR BSW modules, Ethernet drivers and to support the integration team.
